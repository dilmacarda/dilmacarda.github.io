ABOUT

A Modern Brand Built on Detail

Style & Trend is a contemporary fashion label based in Istanbul. We blend clean silhouettes with street energy—designed for daily wear, elevated with premium feel, finished with precise details.

Our Story

Founded in 2026, Style & Trend started with a simple goal: create wardrobe pieces that feel premium, look effortless, and stay relevant across seasons. We believe great style is not loud — it’s clean, confident, and consistent.

We design essentials that work every day, outerwear that defines the silhouette, and sneakers that finish the look. Our collections are intentionally focused: fewer pieces, stronger design decisions, better fit.

We build every drop with a clear direction — fabrics, textures, and shapes that match the brand identity. This keeps everything cohesive: you can mix pieces easily and always look “put together”.
