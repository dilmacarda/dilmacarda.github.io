About Us

We are a fashion brand based in Istanbul, Turkey.

Our Story

Style & Trend started as a simple idea: build a wardrobe that feels premium and modern, without being overdone. We keep designs clean, easy to wear, and consistent — so you can style pieces again and again.

Our collections blend street influence with minimal luxury details. We focus on fit, comfort and finishing, because a good piece should feel right the moment you put it on.

What we care about

Quality First: We keep fabric and stitching standards high. Pieces should hold their form and feel good long-term.

Clean Design: Minimal palette, strong silhouettes, easy styling.

Focused Drops: Fewer pieces, better wardrobe building.
