ABOUT

A Modern Brand Built on Detail

Style & Trend is a contemporary fashion label based in Istanbul. We blend clean silhouettes with street energy—designed for daily wear, elevated with premium feel, and finished with small details that make the difference.

Our Story

Style & Trend was founded in 2026 with one simple goal: create pieces that look effortless and feel premium—without making fashion complicated. We wanted essentials that stay sharp, layers that work across seasons, and silhouettes that feel current without being “too trendy”.

Our aesthetic is minimal and modern. Think clean colors, structured comfort, and wearable shapes that fit your lifestyle—work, weekends, nights out. If you like a wardrobe that always looks “put together”, you’re in the right place.

We build collections in focused drops instead of overwhelming catalogs. That keeps the brand curated and allows more attention to fit, fabric, stitching, and finishing. We’d rather do fewer pieces better.
