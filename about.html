ABOUT

A Modern Brand Built on Detail

Style & Trend is a contemporary fashion label based in Istanbul, blending clean silhouettes with street energy. We focus on premium feel, wearable design, and long-term wardrobe value.

Our Story

Founded in 2026, Style & Trend started with one idea: make fashion look effortless and feel premium—without turning it into something “too formal” or “too trendy”.

We design pieces that work across your day: from casual essentials to refined layers and statement outerwear. Our mood is minimal, our fit is modern, and our details are deliberate.
