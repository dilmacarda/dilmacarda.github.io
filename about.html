About Us

Style & Trend is a contemporary fashion label based in Istanbul. We blend clean silhouettes with street energy—designed for daily wear, elevated with premium feel, finished with precise details.

Our Story

Founded in 2026, Style & Trend started with a simple goal: create wardrobe pieces that feel premium, look effortless, and stay relevant across seasons.

We design essentials that work every day, outerwear that defines the silhouette, and sneakers that finish the look. Our collections are intentionally focused: fewer pieces, stronger design decisions, better fit.

What we focus on

  • Clean design and easy-to-style silhouettes
  • Comfort and fit testing
  • Consistent finishing details
  • Focused drops instead of huge collections

Studio Mood

Minimal workspace, clean direction, and detail-focused design choices.

Atelier

Hands-on refinement and finishing touches for quality consistency.
